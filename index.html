Vincent's Life on Film

Vincent van Gogh decided to become an artist at the age of 27.
That decision would change his life and art history forever.
Vincent Willem van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter who is amongst the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art.
In just over a decade, he created about 2,100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings, most of which date from the last two years of his life.
They include landscapes, still lifes, portraits and self-portraits,
and are characterized by bold colours and dramatic, impuslive and expressive brushwork that contributed to the foundations of modern art.
